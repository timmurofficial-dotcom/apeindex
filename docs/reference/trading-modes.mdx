## Manual Mode (Default)

Every signal is delivered as a Telegram notification with **Enter Trade / Skip** buttons. You decide each trade individually.

- Full control over every execution
- No automatic spending
- Best for users who want to review signals before acting

**When to use:** When you're learning the system, in volatile market conditions, or when you want to apply your own judgment on top of the signal.

## Auto Mode

Signals meeting the enabled patterns are **executed automatically** without user confirmation. Trade size is determined by your per-pattern or global risk settings. A post-execution notification is always sent.

- Hands-free execution
- Never misses a signal cycle
- Sizing governed by your risk configuration

**When to use:** When you trust the patterns, have conservative risk limits set, and want to capture opportunities around the clock.

<Warning>
  Before enabling Auto Mode, make sure your **maximum trade amount** is set to a value you're comfortable losing. The terminal will execute all qualifying signals up to that limit.
</Warning>

## Switching Modes

```
/settings → Trading Mode → Manual / Auto
```

You can switch between modes at any time. Switching to Manual Mode mid-session does not affect already-open positions.

## Per-Pattern Auto Execution

In Auto Mode, you can enable or disable automatic execution **per pattern**. For example, you might want Intersection signals to auto-execute (high confidence) while Contrarian signals require manual approval (higher risk).

```
/settings → Signal Patterns → [Pattern Name] → Auto Execute: On/Off
```

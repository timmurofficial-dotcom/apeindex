## What is ApeIndex?

**ApeIndex** is a full-stack Polymarket intelligence and copy-trading platform built on two tightly integrated products: a **web dashboard** and a **Telegram bot**.

Together they let traders discover top-performing on-chain strategies, follow whale wallets, receive AI-scored signals, and execute trades — all without leaving familiar tools.

## Platform Components

### Web Dashboard — apeindex.trade

The discovery and analytics layer. No account required to browse.

- **Wallet X-Ray** — paste any Polymarket address and get an AI Score, win rate, PnL, and historical position analysis in seconds
- **Traders Leaderboard** — ranked list of all tracked addresses with win rate, PnL, and AI Score. Top performers tagged as WHALE
- **Strategy Vaults** — ranked, backtested trading strategies extracted from on-chain whale behaviour

### Telegram Bot

The execution layer. All trading interaction happens in a single Telegram conversation — no browser extension or external wallet required.

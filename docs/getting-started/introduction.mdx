## What is the Copy-Trading Terminal?

The **ApeIndex** is a real-time, Telegram-native intelligence platform for trading on Polymarket. The terminal monitors on-chain whale activity, detects structural market inefficiencies through five proprietary patterns, scores every signal 1–100, and can execute trades automatically — all without leaving Telegram.

The system processes live market data **every 30 seconds**, maintains per-user position portfolios with unrealised P&L tracking, provides automatic stop-loss and take-profit execution, and delivers daily performance digests.

## Overview

At **09:00 local time** every day, the terminal sends an automated digest to all users with an active monitoring session. The digest aggregates the previous 24 hours of activity from the `signal_history` database table.

## Digest Contents

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Signal Count" icon="bolt">
    Total number of signals fired in the last 24 hours across all active patterns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Average Score" icon="chart-bar">
    Mean composite score across all signals fired — a quick quality indicator.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top 3 Signals" icon="star">
    The three highest-scoring signals from the past day: pattern, market question, score, and outcome.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Realised P&L" icon="dollar-sign">
    Total P&L from positions closed during the day via SL/TP or manual exit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Positions" icon="folder-open">
    Count of positions currently open across all markets.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Example Digest

```
📊 Daily Digest — March 1, 2026

Signals fired: 14  ·  Avg score: 71/100

🏆 Top Signals Yesterday
1. Intersection  ·  "Will Trump sign X by March 5?"  ·  YES  ·  88/100
2. Pre-News      ·  "Fed rate decision March 2026?"  ·  YES  ·  81/100
3. Quick Flip    ·  "Will BTC close above 95k?"      ·  NO   ·  74/100

💰 Realised P&L: +$47.20
📂 Open positions: 3
```

## Digest Timing

The digest is sent at 09:00 **local time** based on the timezone configured in your user settings. If no timezone is set, UTC is used.

Configure your timezone via `/settings → Timezone`.

## Overview

The terminal sends two automated daily digests to all registered users, regardless of whether monitoring is active.

---

## 🌅 Morning Digest — 09:00

Delivered every morning at 09:00 UTC. Summarises your personal activity from the previous 24 hours.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Signal Count" icon="bolt">
    Total signals fired in the last 24h across all active patterns, plus average score.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top 3 Signals" icon="star">
    Highest-scoring signals: pattern, market question, score, suggested outcome.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Realised P&L" icon="dollar-sign">
    Total P&L from positions closed via SL/TP or manual exit during the day.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Positions" icon="folder-open">
    Count of positions currently open across all markets.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Example Morning Digest

```
🌅 Morning Recap — March 14, 2026

Signals fired: 14  ·  Avg score: 71/100

🏆 Top Signals Yesterday
1. 🔀 Intersection  ·  "Will Trump sign X by March 5?"  ·  YES  ·  88/100
2. 📰 Pre-News      ·  "Fed rate decision March 2026?"  ·  YES  ·  81/100
3. 🦑 Big Entry     ·  "Will BTC close above 95k?"      ·  YES  ·  74/100

💰 Realised P&L: +$47.20
📂 Open positions: 3
```

---

## 🌙 Evening Digest — 22:00

Delivered every evening at 22:00 UTC. Shows **global platform statistics** — activity across all users on the platform.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="🌐 Platform Stats" icon="globe">
    Total trades made by ALL users today and total volume traded across the platform.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🏆 Top 3 Traders" icon="trophy">
    Today's top performers by P&L with medals 🥇🥈🥉 — wallet address, trade count, volume, P&L.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Example Evening Digest

```
🌙 Evening Recap
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🌐 Platform stats today
📊 Total trades: 47
💵 Total volume: $3,240

🏆 Top 3 traders today
🥇 0x1a2b...3c4d  —  12 trades
   💵 Volume: $890  ·  📈 P&L: +$124.50

🥈 0x9f3e...7a12  —  8 trades
   💵 Volume: $540  ·  📈 P&L: +$87.20

🥉 0xb4c1...2e89  —  5 trades
   💵 Volume: $320  ·  📈 P&L: +$41.00

Great trading day! See you tomorrow. 🚀
```

Top traders are displayed by **wallet address** (first 6 chars...last 4 chars). If no wallet is on record, shows `User#ID`.

---

## Quiet Day Handling

If no trades occurred during the day, the evening digest sends:
```
Quiet day — no trades today 📊
See you tomorrow!
```

No digest is silently skipped.

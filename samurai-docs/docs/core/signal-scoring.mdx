## How Scoring Works

Every signal receives a **composite score between 1 and 100** before it is delivered. The score is displayed in every notification as a visual progress bar alongside a qualitative label.

```
Score display in notification:
████████░░  78/100  · Strong Signal
```

## Score Labels

| Score Range | Label | Meaning |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| 85–100 | 🔥 **Exceptional** | Highest confidence — multiple confirming factors |
| 70–84 | ✅ **Strong Signal** | High confidence, actionable |
| 50–69 | ⚡ **Moderate** | Worth watching, consider sizing down |
| 30–49 | ⚠️ **Weak Signal** | Low confidence, informational only |
| 1–29 | ❄️ **Noise** | Below threshold for most traders |

## Scoring Factors

The composite score incorporates multiple factors depending on the pattern type:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wallet conviction (Intersection Pattern)">
    The number of followed wallets that have entered the same market increases the score. 2 wallets = baseline. 3+ wallets = significant boost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Volume anomaly strength (Pre-News Pattern)">
    Higher `v24h / v_total` ratios produce higher scores. A 10% ratio is the entry threshold; ratios above 25% approach maximum scores for this component.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Whale profile quality">
    Signals tied to wallets with higher win rates and larger average trade sizes receive a score boost from the whale profile component.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Market liquidity">
    Higher lifetime volume and tighter spreads contribute positively to the score.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Signal freshness">
    Signals generated from very recent on-chain activity (under 30 minutes) score higher than signals based on older data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Score in Notifications

Every signal notification includes:

- The visual bar `█████░░░░░`
- Numeric score (e.g. `63/100`)
- Qualitative label
- The pattern that fired
- Market question and suggested outcome

Use the score to prioritise which signals to act on in Manual Mode, or set a minimum score threshold in Auto Mode settings.
